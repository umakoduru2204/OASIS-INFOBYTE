HARLEY DAVIDSON X440

DESCRIPTION

The H-D X440 is the single cylinder Harley-Davidson, reimagined. With unmistakable power and presence along with a nimble, comfortable ride, the H-D X440 is designed to bring a whole new generation into the Harley-Davidson family.

OVERVIEW
All-new 440cc oil cooled engine Powerful signature Harley-Davidson exhaust note Nimble, agile handling

KEY FEATURES

01 THE HEART OF THE X440 The all-new 440cc oil cooled engine makes a classic retro design statement and exudes power as well as ample low and mid range torque.The 6-speed transmission offers gearing that is optimized for both highway cruising and for city commuting.

02 BUILT FOR COMFORT AND CONFIDENCE The frame boasts Harley-Davidson’s signature wide and flat handlebars for a commanding rider position. The comfortable saddle style rider seat is perfect for long rides, while optimised chassis geometry ensures a stable ride at high speeds. The pillion seat and legroom combine with optimized pillion grab-rails ensure best-in-class passenger comfort.
